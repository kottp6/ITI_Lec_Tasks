1- Backlinks 
-> Backlinks play a crucial role in Search Engine Optimization (SEO) because they
signal to search engines like Google that other websites find your content valuable and worth linking to

2-What’re meta tags? And what’re its benefits and uses? 
They provide metadata about the webpage, such as its title, description, keywords, 
and other information that helps search engines and browsers understand the content and context of the page

3-What are search engines? And how it work?
Search engines are web-based tools that help users find information on the internet by searching for specific keywords or phrases

4-What’s SEO?
SEO stands for Search Engine Optimization . It is the practice of improving a website's visibility and ranking on search engine results pages
